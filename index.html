Ruby Retreat!

27-30 March 2020 - Mt Cheeseman, New Zealand

What is a Ruby Retreat?
Ruby Retreat is an unconference run by and for the Ruby programming language community.
Picture yourself in the mountains with your smartest friends. The air is clear and so is your mind. There's no limit to what you can achieve here.
  • Scheming? Certainly.
  • Coding? It's likely.
  • Enlightenment? Maybe.
  • Drinking? *Not* alcohol. Ruby Retreat is alcohol free.
What do I do at a Ruby Retreat?
This will be the very first Ruby Retreat, although it is the sequel to Rails Camp. It's a low-structure unconference; activities are driven by the community.
Some suggestions:
  • Work on Ruby projects.
  • Share your work with others.
  • Talk through gnarly problems with thoughtful, friendly folks.
  • Connect with the Ruby community.
  • Follow your dreams.
Should I come to Ruby Retreat?
Yes. Buy a ticket here.
When should I arrive to, and leave from, Christchurch Airport?
Please arrive before 3PM on Friday, 27th March. You are more than welcome to arrive early!
There is a lot of beauty in the Canterbury region, and it's worth seeing.
Barring any unforeseen circumstances out of our control, it's safe to book flights to leave after 12PM on Monday, 30th March.
Again, it'd be even better to extend your trip and see the region, or even the entire South Island.
What do I need to bring?
Some bedding. There are mattresses and pillows and 1 blanket per bunk but no sheets, duvets etc so you will need to bring these, or sleeping bags along with pillow cases.
